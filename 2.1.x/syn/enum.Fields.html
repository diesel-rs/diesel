Variants§

§

Named(FieldsNamed)

Named fields of a struct or struct variant such as Point { x: f64, y: f64 }.

§

Unnamed(FieldsUnnamed)

Unnamed fields of a tuple struct or tuple variant such as Some(T).

§

Unit

Unit struct or unit variant such as None.
