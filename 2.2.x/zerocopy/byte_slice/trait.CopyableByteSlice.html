A ByteSlice which can be copied without violating dereference stability.

§Safety

If B: CopyableByteSlice, then the dereference stability properties required by ByteSlice (see that trait’s safety documentation) do not only hold regarding two calls to b.deref() or b.deref_mut(), but also hold regarding c.deref() or c.deref_mut(), where c is produced by copying b.

Dyn Compatibility§

This trait is not dyn compatible.

In older versions of Rust, dyn compatibility was called "object safety", so this trait is not object safe.
